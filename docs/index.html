About the App

Dictation5x5 is a professional-grade dictation app that provides real-time speech-to-text transcription. Perfect for medical professionals, the app uses advanced speech recognition technology to accurately transcribe your voice into text.

Features

  • Real-time speech-to-text transcription
  • Secure authentication (Sign in with Apple, Google, or Email)
  • Local storage of recordings and transcripts
  • Recording history and playback
  • Optimized for medical terminology
