Support & Help

Welcome to the Dictation5x5 support page. Find answers to common questions and get help with the app.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: How do I sign in to the app?
A: You can sign in using Sign in with Apple, Google Sign-In, or create an account with your email and password. Tap the sign-in option you prefer on the login screen.
Q: How do I start recording?
A: After signing in, tap the "Start" button on the main screen. The app will begin recording audio from your device's microphone and display transcriptions in real-time.
Q: Where are my recordings stored?
A: All audio recordings and transcripts are stored locally on your device. They are not uploaded to our servers. You can view your recording history by tapping the history icon (clock) in the bottom right corner.
Q: How do I delete my account?
A: Go to Settings → Account (tap the person icon) → Actions → Delete Account. You'll be asked to confirm before your account is permanently deleted.
Q: Can I use this app offline?
A: The app requires an internet connection to transcribe audio using Google Cloud Speech-to-Text API. However, you can view and play back previously recorded sessions offline.
Q: Is my data secure?
A: Yes. Audio recordings are processed in real-time and stored only on your device. Account information is securely stored using Firebase Authentication. See our Privacy Policy for more details.
Q: How accurate is the transcription?
A: Transcription accuracy depends on several factors including audio quality, background noise, speaking clarity, and accent. The app uses Google Cloud Speech-to-Text API with medical model optimization for better accuracy with medical terminology.
Q: Can I edit transcripts?
A: Currently, transcripts are read-only. You can copy the text and edit it in another app. We're working on adding in-app editing features in future updates.
Q: How do I change the server URL?
A: Go to Settings → Server Configuration. You can enter a custom WebSocket server URL or use one of the preset options. Note: This is typically only needed for advanced users or developers.

Troubleshooting

App won't connect to server

  • Check your internet connection
  • Verify the server URL in Settings is correct
  • Try restarting the app
  • If using Cloud Run, ensure the service is running

Microphone not working

  • Check that microphone permissions are enabled in iOS Settings → Dictation5x5
  • Ensure no other app is using the microphone
  • Try restarting the app
  • Check that your device's microphone is not blocked or damaged

Transcription not appearing

  • Ensure you're connected to the internet
  • Check that the server is running and accessible
  • Try stopping and starting the recording again
  • Check the connection status in Settings

Sign in not working

  • Check your internet connection
  • Verify your email and password are correct
  • If using Sign in with Apple, ensure it's enabled on your device
  • If using Google Sign-In, ensure you're signed in to Google on your device
  • Try signing out and signing back in

Audio playback not working

  • Check your device volume
  • Ensure audio files weren't deleted
  • Try restarting the app
  • Check that your device's speakers are working
