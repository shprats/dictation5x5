Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2025

Dictation5x5 ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address, display name (if provided), and authentication credentials. If you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive basic profile information from those services.
  • Audio Recordings: The App records audio from your device's microphone to provide speech-to-text transcription services. Audio data is processed in real-time and stored locally on your device.
  • Transcripts: Transcribed text is stored locally on your device for your reference and playback.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: We may collect information about how you use the App, including session duration, features used, and error logs.
  • Device Information: We may collect device-specific information such as device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App's transcription services
  • Process and transcribe your audio recordings using third-party speech recognition services
  • Authenticate your identity and manage your account
  • Respond to your support requests and inquiries
  • Send you important updates about the App (if you opt in)
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues

3. Third-Party Services

3.1 Google Cloud Speech-to-Text API

We use Google Cloud Speech-to-Text API to transcribe your audio recordings. When you use the App, your audio data is sent to Google's servers for processing. Google's use of your data is governed by their Privacy Policy. We do not store your audio recordings on our servers; they are processed in real-time and stored only locally on your device.

3.2 Firebase Authentication

We use Firebase Authentication (provided by Google) to manage user accounts and authentication. This service processes your authentication credentials and basic profile information. Firebase's use of your data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

3.3 Sign in with Apple

If you choose to sign in with Apple, Apple processes your authentication. Apple's use of your data is governed by their Privacy Policy.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Audio recordings and transcripts are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data unless you explicitly share it with us.
  • Cloud Storage: Your account information (email, display name) is stored securely in Firebase's cloud infrastructure.
  • Security Measures: We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including encryption in transit and at rest.

5. Data Retention

  • Account Information: We retain your account information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services.
  • Local Data: Audio recordings and transcripts stored on your device remain on your device until you delete them or uninstall the App.
  • Deletion: When you delete your account, we will delete your account information from our systems. Local data on your device will remain until you delete it or uninstall the App.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your information:

  • Access: You can access your account information through the App's settings.
  • Correction: You can update your account information at any time through the App.
  • Deletion: You can delete your account at any time through the App's settings. This will permanently delete your account information from our systems.
  • Local Data: You can delete audio recordings and transcripts directly from the App at any time.
  • Opt-Out: You can stop using the App and delete your account at any time.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have data protection laws that differ from those in your country. By using the App, you consent to the transfer of your information to these countries.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
